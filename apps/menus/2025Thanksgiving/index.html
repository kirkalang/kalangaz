Schedule

Day #1 - Nov 25th

  • Bread. Make and bake 2 loaves.
  • Make Turkey Stock
  • Pie Dough. Make the dough. Cover with plastic wrap and store in the fridge.

Day #2 - Nov 26th

  • 9:00 am. Drive up to Surprise.
  • Afternoon. Bread Stuffing. Toast and cut bread. Chop and mix the dressing.
  • Afternoon. Apple Dumplings. If we decide on these then prep apples. Roll out dough. Wrap apples in dough and get into the fridge.
  • AfternoonPumpkin Pie. Make pie custard. Roll out pie dough. Cook the pie and store in fridge.
  • EveningBreakfast French Toast. Cut bread and pour egg mixture over. Prepare apples and place on top of bread. Cover with cinnamon sugar mixture. Place in fridge over night.
  • Evening. Ambrosia. Drain cans of fruit in fridge over night.
  • Pick up Mary from airport Wednesday night

Day #3 - Thanksgiving

  • Morning. French Toast. Bake french toast. Cook bacon. Prepare fruit salad.
  • Morning. Orange Bowknots. Make the dough. Form the knots and let them sit out to rise.
  • 10:00 am. Turkey. Remove from cooler and bag. Make turkeyt injection. Cool injection liquid.
  • 11:00 am. Turkey. Stuff turkey with apples, sage and garlic. Tie turkey. Inject turkey.
  • 11:00 am. Ambrosia. Make the ambrosia. Cover and store in fridge or cooler.
  • 11:00 am. Turkey. Place steel into oven and turn oven on to 500°.
  • 12:00 pm. Turkey. Place turkey into oven. Set oven to 300°
  • 1:00 pm. Sweet Potatoes. Fully prepare potatoes and then put to the side. Reheat in hot oven just before serving. Prepare the candied pecans but do not add to dish until just prior to serving.
  • 2:00 pm. Mashed Potatoes. Cut and cook potatoes. Mash and mix with ~3/4 of the butter and milk. Cover and store.
  • 2:00 pm. Turkey. Check temperature at multiple locations. Thighs. Breasts.
  • 2:30 pm. Brussels Sprouts. Make the balsamic reduction.
  • 3:00 pm. Brussels Sprouts. Clean and cut. Mix with oil and place onto 1 or 2 baking sheets. Keep in a single layer with cut side down.
  • 3:00 pm. Bread Stuffing. Place covered dressing into 350° oven. Cook to 150° internally.
  • 3:45 pm. Bread Stuffing. At 150° internal, remove foil cover. Cook for another 15 - 20 mins, until nice brown crust formed on top.
  • ~3:30 - 4:00 pm.Turkey. Remove turkey from oven when deepest part of breast measures 150° and leg measures 165°
    • After Removing Turkey. Increase both ovens temperature to 400°.
  • After Turkey. Brussels Sprouts. Place into 400° oven. Once down move to serving dish. Drizzle with balsamic g
  • After Turkey. Gravy. Make the gravy.
  • After Turkey. Mashed Potatoes. Reheat in oven. Heat remaining butter and milk in microwave. Mix into reheated potatoes until reach desired texture.
  • After Turkey. Sweet Potatoes. Reheat in hot. Garnish with candied pecans just prior to serving.
